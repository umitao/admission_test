Black Hole of Curiosity

 A black hole is a region of spacetime exhibiting gravitational acceleration so strong that nothing —no particles or even electromagnetic radiation such as light—can escape from it.

Curiosity (from Latin cūriōsitās, "careful, diligent, curious") is a quality related to inquisitive thinking such as exploration, investigation, and learning, evident by observation in humans and other animals.

Learning and desire to acquire new skills and knowledge is directly driven by curiosity. Therefore, we can say the best learners have a black hole of curiosity within their mind.
